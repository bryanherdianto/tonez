Tonez

Sentiment Analysis Demo

Tonez loads a pre-trained model straight into your browser with TensorFlow.js and reads the tone of a short restaurant review as a score between 0 and 1. Training happens server-side using Python, and the resulting model runs entirely client-side.

The model is trained on Yelp reviews truncated to a maximum of 20 words, using only the 2000 most common words found in the reviews. Try it out for yourself below.

About the Model

Dataset

1,000 labeled Yelp restaurant reviews, split between positive and negative sentiment.

Preprocessing

Reviews are lowercased and tokenized using the 2,000 most frequent words in the dataset, then padded or truncated to 20 tokens.

Training

Trained for 30 epochs with the Adam optimizer and binary cross-entropy loss, reaching around 99% training accuracy and 75% validation accuracy.

Trained in Python with TensorFlow and Keras, then converted to TensorFlow.js so it runs entirely in your browser.
